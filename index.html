5 Simple and Effective Home Remedies

  • Drink Plenty of Warm Water and Herbal Teas: Ginger, lemon, garlic soothe your throat and boost immunity.
  • Rest Well and Sleep Enough: Healing happens in sleep—don’t push yourself.
  • Eat Natural Foods: Oranges, mangoes, leafy greens strengthen your immune system.
  • Use Honey and Lemon: Mix in warm water to calm cough and sore throat.
  • Stay in a Warm Environment: Keep chest and feet warm, sunbathe 20 mins daily.
